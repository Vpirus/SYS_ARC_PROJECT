Our Story & Mission

AgriGo connects consumers directly with local farmers, promoting sustainable agriculture and ensuring fair prices for both farmers and customers. We're building a better food system, one harvest at a time.

Our Mission

To create a sustainable marketplace that connects local farmers directly with consumers, eliminating middlemen and ensuring fair prices for quality produce. We aim to support small-scale farming, promote sustainable agriculture, and make fresh, healthy food accessible to all.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every community has access to locally grown, fresh produce, where farmers receive fair compensation for their hard work, and where sustainable agricultural practices are the norm. We strive to build a food system that benefits people and the planet.

How We Started

AgriGo began in 2020 when a group of agricultural enthusiasts saw the struggles of local farmers trying to sell their produce at fair prices. Middlemen were taking significant cuts, and consumers were paying high prices for produce that wasn't always fresh.

We started with just five farmers in a small community, creating a simple online platform where they could sell directly to nearby residents. The response was overwhelming – consumers loved the fresh produce and the connection to their local farmers, while farmers appreciated the fair prices and direct relationship with their customers.

Today, AgriGo works with hundreds of farmers across the region, supplying fresh, organic produce to thousands of households. Our platform has expanded to include educational resources about sustainable farming and seasonal eating, fostering a community that cares about where their food comes from.
